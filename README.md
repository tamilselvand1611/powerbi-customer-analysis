# Customer Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## Tool
- Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
- Superstore Dataset (Kaggle)

## Project Description
Created an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze and visualize sales and profit performance across regions, segments, categories, and customers. 
The project highlights key performance indicators, trends, and actionable insights to support business decision-making.

## Key Insights

### Overall Performance
- Total Sales: 2.30M
- Total Profit: 286.40K
- Customer Count: 793
- Profit Margin: 12.5%

### Sales by Region
- West and East regions contribute the highest share of sales.
- The Central region shows weaker performance compared to others.

### Sales by Segment
- The Consumer segment generates the largest portion of profit.
- Corporate and Home Office segments follow with moderate contributions.

### Sales by Category
- Technology and Furniture lead in total sales.
- Office Supplies contribute comparatively less.

### Sales Trend
- Sales show steady growth over time.
- Seasonal variations are observed, with spikes in certain months.

### Sales by Sub-Category
- Phones and Copiers are top-performing products.
- Tables and Bookcases record lower or negative profits.

## Key Features
- KPI cards for Sales, Profit, Customer Count, and Profit Margin
- Regional, segment, and customer-level analysis
- Time-series trend analysis
- Interactive slicers for dynamic exploration
